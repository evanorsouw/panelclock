

OLIMEX 32U4 REV.B Arduino board
-------------------------------
LEDs

PE6 - D7  - LED1    
PB5 - D9  - LED2
PD5 -     - Green LED  TxLED
PB0 - D17 - Yellow LED (Rx)

Buttons:
PE2   - HWB Button
Reset - Reset
